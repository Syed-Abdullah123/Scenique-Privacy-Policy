Request Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your Scenique account and remove all associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Delete Your Account

You have two options to delete your account:

Option 1: In-App Account Deletion

If you have the Scenique app installed, go to:

  • Open the **Scenique App**
  • Go to **Profile > Settings**
  • Select **Delete My Account**
  • Confirm your request

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.

Option 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion by emailing us at:

abdullah.works.dev@gmail.com

In your email, please include:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your username
  • A request to delete your account

Important Notes

- Account deletion is **permanent** and cannot be undone.

- Some minimal data (e.g., legal records) may be retained for compliance reasons.
